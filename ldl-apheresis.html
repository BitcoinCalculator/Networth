Kaneka Liposorber LA-15 — Setup & Priming Tips
Regular Priming Supplies
NS 1000 mL × 3
LR with Heparin 1000 mL × 1
LR 1000 mL × 3
Hypertonic 5% Saline 500 mL × 1 (×2 if processing > 3000 mL)
Albumin Priming Supplies
NS 1000 mL × 3
LR 1000 mL × 4
Heparinized Albumin 500 mL × 2
Hypertonic 5% Saline 500 mL × 1
Tubing Setup (5 steps)
1. Turn on machine, follow prompts for machine test & tubing setup.
2. Connect tubing only as directed.
3. Double check: 11 valves, 7 connections, 7 transducers.
4. Connect separator and columns as directed.
5. Attach drain bag.
Initial Prime (steps 6–11)
6. Connect heparin 20 mL syringe infusion.
7. Connect 2 bags of NS 1000 mL (bridged) → FD1 and FD2.
8. Connect Hypertonic 5% Saline → FD3.
9. Fill chambers — make sure NO AIR in tubing above chamber.
10. 1st prime.
11. Prime arterial line portion as instructed.
Regular Prime (after step 11)
1. Connect 1 bag of heparinized LR → FD1 and FD2.
2. 2nd prime.
3. Empty drain bag.
Albumin Prime (after step 11)
1. Connect 1 bag of LR 1000 mL → FD1 and FD2.
2. 2nd prime.
3. Empty drain bag.
4. Connect 2 bottles heparinized albumin (1 → FD1, 1 → FD2) — filter needle for vent.
5. 3rd prime — Albumin.
Treatment Start
1. Bridge 2–3 bags of LR 1000 mL together → FD2.
2. Program machine: plasma volume to process + heparin flow rate.
3. Confirm clamp to right of V11 is clamped.
4. Start treatment.
